LazerTank

A capture the flag puzzle game

Available Commands:

Shoot - space bar

Fire the tank's laser once, cummulatively

Shoot multiple times - numeric keys

Fire the tank's laser the specific number of times

Move - arrow keys

Drive the tank in the direction of the arrow key

Move to a specific square - mouse left click

Move the tank to the mouse position. To succeed it must be possible for the tank to move to the square that the mouse is pointing to. The tank will find the shortest path to the given square

Move along a specific path - mouse drag

Dictate a path for the tank by moving the mouse while holding down the left mouse button

Capture the flag - c

Press c to move the tank to the flag. Works if the tank can find a possible path to it

Pause - Ctrl

Hold down the Ctrl key to pause. Focus switches from the last pending move to the tank so that by default, entering moves while paused replaces any outstanding moves. While paused, focus can be changed using the Home and End keys

Undo - backspace

Erase the last pending move. Ctrl-backspace erases all pending moves. This undoes pending tank movement - it does not undo completed tank moves. To undo completed moves, use Auto Replay

Speed control - s

Typing the s key toggles the tank's speed between high and low. The default is low. High speed is useful if/when you find yourself waiting for the tank to catch up. In low speed, the tank will make adjustments to it speed as the number of outstanding moves increases

Menu - Esc or right mouse click

Open the menu. Using the right mouse click to open adds some additional context for the square under the mouse cursor

Select Level - Alt L

Choose a level to open

Auto Replay - Alt R

Watch a replay of completed moves. Entering commands during replay (such as Esc) allows game play to resume from the current point in time. This is useful for undoing the remaining committed moves. Selecting replay before any moves have been entered replays the last time the level was succesfully completed

Game Pieces

Tank You control this vehicle by driving it and shooting its lazer
Tile A pushable piece. Pushing this onto water creates a bridge. A tile is pushed either by driving into it or by shooting it. Pieces can not be pushed into a square occupied by another piece
Bridge A tile submerged in water. Pieces can move over bridge squares
Flag Moving the tank onto the flag completes the level
Stone An indestructable, immovable barrier
Wood Cannot be pushed, though it can be damaged by shooting it. Shooting it once damages it. Shooting damaged wood destroys it
Damaged
Wood		 Cannot be pushed, though shooting it destroys it. Destroying it enables movement within its square
Cannon A cannon will shoot at the tank whenever it moves within its line of sight. Cannons do not shoot through mirrors. A cannon is destroyed by shooting into its muzzle. A cannon can be pushed on its left, right and back sides
Tile
Mirror		 Reflects the tank's lazer when shot into either glass side. Can be pushed on its non-reflective sides
Stone
Mirror		 An immovable barrier that reflects the tank's lazer when shot into either glass side
Slit Cannot be moved, but shots pass through its glass core
Water The tank cannot move on water, though pushing a tile into water creates a bridge. Pushing other pieces into water causes them to sink effectively destroying them
Dirt An unoccuppied square. Has freedom of movement
